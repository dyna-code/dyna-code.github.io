--- title: "Incident Response Case Studies" excerpt: "" collection: portfolio --- My incident response case studies cover broad range of cyber security incidents. These case studies are designed to provide a high-level overview of the incident, the response, and the lessons learned.
  • Case Study 1: Yahoo Data Breach

    A data breach impacting Yahoo, including delayed detection, Verizon's response efforts, and lessons learned about cybersecurity practices.

  • Case Study 2: Sands Corp. Ransomware Attack

    A state-aligned cyberattack on Las Vegas Sands Corp., including the response, financial impact, and opportunities for improvement. My report highlights containment efforts, operational disruptions, and how to strengthen future cybersecurity defenses.

  • Case Study 3: Equifax Technical Debt

    The 2017 Equifax data breach, analyses including the exploitation of an Apache Spark vulnerability, the company’s inadequate incident response, and its consequences. Insights into the differences between SIEM and SOAR technologies.

  • Case Study 4: STUXNET IOT Attack

    A comprehensive examination of the STUXNET malware, focusing on its targeted disruption of Iran’s nuclear facilities, the global spread of its variants, and the challenges in controlling advanced cyber weapons. Key lessons emphasize the enhanced regulation of source code distribution and cybersecurity protocols.
