--- title: "Incident Response Case Studies" excerpt: "" collection: portfolio --- My incident response case studies cover broad arange of cyber security incidents. These case studies are designed to provide a high-level overview of the incident, the response, and the lessons learned.
  • Case Study 1: Yahoo Data Breach

    A detailed analysis of a data breach impacting Yahoo, including delayed detection, Verizon's response efforts, and lessons learned about cybersecurity practices.

  • Case Study 2: Sands Corp. Ransomware Attack

    A detailed analysis of a state-aligned cyberattack on Las Vegas Sands Corp., including the response, financial impact, and opportunities for improvement. The report highlights containment efforts, operational disruptions, and lessons learned to strengthen future cybersecurity defenses.

  • Case Study 3: Equifax Technical Debt

    A detailed analysis of the 2017 Equifax data breach, including the exploitation of a known vulnerability, the company’s inadequate incident response, and its consequences. Insights into the differences between SIEM and SOAR technologies and a comparison with the NASA breach highlight shared vulnerabilities and essential lessons for improving cybersecurity practices.

  • Case Study 4: STUXNET IOT

    A comprehensive examination of the STUXNET malware, focusing on its targeted disruption of Iran’s nuclear facilities, the global spread of its variants, and the challenges in controlling advanced cyber weapons. The analysis highlights response actions, containment measures, and opportunities for improvement, including enhanced regulation of source code and cybersecurity protocols. Key lessons learned emphasize the critical need for robust control over malware development and distribution.
